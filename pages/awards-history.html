  • Minimum 2 characters are required.
  • Search is case-insensitive.
  • Search any part of the word, beginning, ending, or character(s) in the middle of the word. For example:
    • To find "Nanofiber", search for Nano or ber or nofi.
  • Separate multiple search terms with at least one space. Results are found if each term matches some part of a word. For example:
    • Searching for diese, haust, sel exha or exha sel will all yield results where "Diesel" is followed by "Exhaust" or vice-versa.
    • Searching for Diesel Exhaust not_here_at_all fails if "Diesel" and "Exhaust" exist but "not_here_at_all" is missing.
  • Exact searches should be surrounded by double quotes. For example, to find "Prosthetic Knee" precisely, search for "Prosthetic Knee" or "prosthetic knee". On the other hand:
    • "Knee Prosthetic" fails because "Prosthetic" appears first.
    • "Prosthetic   Knee" fails because there is only one space between "Prosthetic" and "Knee"
  • Search results are returned as you type.
